What is MinimaTXT?

MinimaTXT is a lightweight and blazing fast iOS app for creating, editing, and managing plain .txt files directly on your iPhone.

  • Minimalist design, no distractions
  • Lightning-fast file opening and saving
  • Reliable Undo / Redo support
  • Automatic saves on app backgrounding or closing
  • Privacy-first: all data stays on your device, no servers, no tracking
  • Local storage only â€” no third-party cloud, no Apple/iCloud access
  • Minimal external dependencies (just ZIPFoundation), maximum reliability
  • Full-text search with instant highlighting
  • Import and export your text files easily
  • Batch export as a ZIP archive
  • Native iOS 17+ experience, built with SwiftUI
