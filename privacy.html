Privacy Policy

MinimaTXT respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1️⃣ Data Collection

MinimaTXT does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

2️⃣ File Storage and Security

  • All documents you create or edit are stored locally on your device, inside the application’s private storage (sandboxed Documents directory).
  • Files are protected using iOS system encryption (NSFileProtectionComplete):
    • Encrypted and inaccessible while your device is locked;
    • Decrypted only after authentication (such as Face ID, passcode).
  • MinimaTXT does not backup, upload or sync your files to any server or cloud.

3️⃣ Sharing and Export

You may choose to export or share your text files using standard iOS share sheets. When sharing, a copy of the file is handed over to the selected target app or contact. MinimaTXT does not track or store any information about these shares.

4️⃣ Third Parties

MinimaTXT does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. Your activity stays fully within the app.

5️⃣ User Consent

By using this app, you agree that:

  • You use the app at your own risk.
  • All data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.
  • The developer does not guarantee the safety, integrity, or long-term availability of any data or files created or edited in this app.
  • It is your responsibility to maintain backups of your data outside the app.
  • The developer shall not be held liable for any data loss, corruption, or other damages arising from the use of this app.
