Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how MinimaTXT handles your information. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your data.

1. No Data Collection

MinimaTXT does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. This includes, but is not limited to: names, email addresses, IP addresses, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

The app does not use cookies, advertising, tracking tools, or third-party SDKs of any kind.

2. Local File Storage

All documents you create or edit using MinimaTXT are stored locally on your device, inside the app’s private storage. Your files are encrypted by the iOS system and are inaccessible while the device is locked. The app does not back up, upload, or sync your files to any server or cloud.

Please note: deleting the app will also delete your local files unless you have manually exported or backed them up.

3. Sharing and Exporting Files

You may choose to export or share your files using standard iOS sharing tools. When you do, a copy of the selected file is passed to the target app or service. MinimaTXT does not track or store any information about sharing activity.
