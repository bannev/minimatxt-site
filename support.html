Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does MinimaTXT sync with iCloud?
    No. All your files are stored locally on your iPhone. No cloud, no servers.
  • Can I import .txt files?
    Yes — you can import via menu in the upper right corner.
  • Can I export my files?
    Yes — export individual or multiple files or Export All as a ZIP archive.
  • Does it support Markdown?
    No. MinimaTXT is focused on plain text only, by design.
  • The app crashed — what should I do?
    Please email us with details (what you were doing, which file, etc.) and we’ll look into it.

We usually respond within 48 hours.
